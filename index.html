ABOUT ENVI

ENVI (Encoded Virtual Intelligence) is the festival of technology, thought and innovation conducted by the Department of Computer Science of St. Joseph's College (Autonomous), Devagiri, Calicut. With a wide variety of events, having participants from all over the nation, ENVI is the platform for the students to develop and showcase technical proficiency as well as artistic talents. ENVI came into reality on 25th January 2018. ENVI has a significant presence on the social media platforms and has reached a large audience. The Instagram page of ENVI has about 3000 followers and 3,00,000 impressions. To test the technical knowledge of the students, events such as Coding, Debugging, ReAssembling, App challenge, Designing are being conducted. Also competitions in Gaming, Treasure hunt, and quizzes are held. IT Exhibitions are being conducted to impart awareness about the latest tech innovations and trends. One of the highlights of ENVI 5 event is ‘Hackathon’, where students form teams to brainstorm and create innovative tech solutions to real-world problems. The pro-show Gravity has always been a visually stunning and captivating performance. ENVI 5 aims to conduct the program nationwide, in all its glory
